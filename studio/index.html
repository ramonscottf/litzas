How it works
  1. Copy prompt on a pizza → paste it into your image generator, and attach one of the finished pizza photos as the style reference.
  2. Save the image it makes to your iPad.
  3. Back here, tap Add photo on that item, pick the image. Pizzas get the white background removed; plated dishes (salads, spaghetti, lasagna) get cropped to their round plate — check the preview.
  4. Tap Upload. It lands in R2 and shows on the live menu on the next refresh. No rebuild.
Photos go to the litzas-menu R2 bucket and serve straight onto litzas.wickowaypoint.com.
